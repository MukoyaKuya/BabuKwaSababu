Meet Babu

Babu Owino (born Paul Ongili Owino) is a selfless leader, an intellectual powerhouse, and a dedicated advocate for the people of Kenya. With a background in Actuarial Science and Law, he brings a unique blend of strategic thinking and justice-driven action.

Since his entry into politics, Babu has been at the forefront of the struggle for youth empowerment, equitable education, and constitutional integrity. He believes that a city and a nation can only thrive when the most vulnerable are uplifted.

He is not just running for office; he is running to spark a revolution of hope across every county.
